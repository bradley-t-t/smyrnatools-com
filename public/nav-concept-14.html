Concept 14

Breadcrumb Nav

No traditional nav menu. Navigation is driven entirely by interactive breadcrumbs in a secondary bar. Each breadcrumb segment is clickable and opens a dropdown showing sibling items at that level.


Clicking "Home" shows all categories. Clicking a category shows its items. The breadcrumb trail updates as you drill down. Maximum content area — navigation only appears when you interact with the breadcrumbs.
