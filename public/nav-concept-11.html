Vertical Tabs Sidebar

A sidebar where each group is a vertical tab on the left edge. Clicking a tab highlights it and reveals that group's items in the adjacent sidebar panel. Dashboard sits as a standalone item at the top.


Two-level sidebar navigation — the narrow icon strip handles categories while the wider panel shows individual items. Keeps the layout compact and organized.
