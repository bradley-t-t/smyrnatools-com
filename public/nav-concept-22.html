Concept 22 — Dashboard Hub

Full-page dashboard navigation with category cards

No traditional sidebar or hamburger menu. The main view IS the navigation, presented as a home-screen-style dashboard with rich category cards. Each card shows its items inline, and everything is clickable.

  • Full-page card grid replaces traditional navigation
  • Category cards with gradient backgrounds and lift-on-hover
  • Inline item lists with counts and hover arrows
  • Quick-access chips for common actions
  • Sticky frosted-glass header with search
  • Color-coded categories (blue, purple, green, red)
  • Region selector and notification bell in header
  • Smooth dark/light theme transition
