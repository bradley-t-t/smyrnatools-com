Concept 23 — Glassmorphism Floating Nav

Frosted-glass navigation panel floating over gradient mesh content

A modern glassmorphism approach where the navigation panel floats over the page content with a frosted-glass effect. The narrow 200px panel uses backdrop-filter blur with subtle borders, and the background features a gradient mesh that is visible through the translucent nav.

  • Frosted-glass panel with backdrop-filter blur
  • Floating panel with rounded corners and shadow
  • Gradient mesh background visible through nav
  • Narrow 200px width for minimal intrusion
  • Active item indicator with accent bar
  • Thin separators between groups
  • Demo glass stat cards showing the effect on content
  • Full dark/light theme with mesh color adaptation
  • Search bar expands on focus
